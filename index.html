Ram Mandir

The BHUMI PUJAN of Ram mandir was held on 5thOctober 2020. The temple was inaugurated on 22ndJanuary 2024 after a pran pratishtha ceremony.

The Babri Masjid was demolished by Hindu nationalists on 6th December 1992 leading to widespread riots. The supreme court of India ruled in favor of constructing a Ram mandir at disputed site. The Ram Janmabhoomi Teerth Kshetra Trust was established to oversee temple construction.

Our Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi served as Mukhya Yajaman of the rituals and performed pran pratishtha of the temple.

On the first day of it's opening, the temple received the rush of over half a million visitors. It was a great day for all Indians.

XPoSat

XPoSat is India's first dedicated polarimetry mission to study various dynamics of bright astronomical X-ray sourses in extreme conditions. The spacecraft will carry two payloads in a low earth orbit. It will measure the degree and angle of polarization in medium X-ray energy range 8-30keV photons of astronomical origin. It was launched on 1stJanuary 2024.

Paralympics 2024



India made history in Paralympics held in Paris from 28th August 2024 to 8thSeptember 2024.

India won a total of 29 medals including 7 gold medals, 9 silver medals and 13 bronze medals and surpassed the previous record of winning 19 medals at Tokyo paralympics 2020.

Avani Lekhra became the first Indian woman to win two gold medals in Paralympics,defending her title in women's 10m air rifle standing SH1(Shooters with minimum physical impairement) event with a world record score.

Sumit Antil won a gold medal in men's Javelin throw F64 with a stunning throw of 70.59m.

Harvinder Singh won a medal in archery, Dharambir Nain won gold in men's club throw F51 event, Praveen Kumar won a gold in men's high jump T64 event and Navdeep Singh won a gold in men's Jvelin throw F41 event.

C295 Aircraft

Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi on 28thOctober 2024 inaugurated a plant in Vadodra where Tata Advanced Systems Ltd(TASL) will manufacture C-295 aircraft for Indian Air Force.Spanish Prime Minister Pedro Sanchez was also present in Gujarat,with the two leaders having laid the foundation stone of Final Assembly Line plant in October 2022.

The C-295 was originally manufactured by a Spanish aircraft manufacturer named Construcciones Aeronauticas SA.

It is a transport aircraft with a 5-10 tonne capacity and a maximum speed of 480kmph. It has a rear ramp door for quick reaction and para-dropping of troops and cargo. Short take-off and landing from semi-prepared surfaces are some other features.
